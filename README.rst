ThreeArmJS
==========

A simple nonstationary 3-arm bandit task modeled after `Huang et al. (2017) <https://doi.org/10.1016/j.biopsych.2017.07.007>`_ and `Suzuki et al. (2019) <https://doi.org/10.1101/730614>`_.
